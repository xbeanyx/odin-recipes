Seafood Risotto Recipe

Description

One thing Italy is well known and famous for is its risotto. Risotto is a beloved creamy Italian dish made with a specific type of short-grain white rice.

Ingredients Required:

  • WHite Rice
  • Prawns
  • Clams
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Rice
  2. Fry Seafood
  3. Add Cheese and mix everything
